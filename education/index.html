K-12 Education

We participate in several programs in K-12 STEM education at NYU. This includes the Applied Research Innovations in Science and Engineering (ARISE) program, which offers laboratory research experience over the summer for high-school students living in New York City. We also co-created the summer NYU K-12 Machine Learning summer program and coordinated its organization between 2019 and 2022.
